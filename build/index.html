How to play

Pims are stuck in pimbo—help them out by directing them to collect the parts they need to become whole again.

Ghosts will need to collect skulls to claim a body, and then they'll become zombies! Then collect the glowing life that matches the color of its soul to bring the Pim back to it's true form.

The Pims won't know what's around them when they aren't themselves, so you'll need to direct them. Click the ghost or zombie to prime (!) them, and to direct them according to your control. They won't pay attention for long, and you must keep nudging them.

Clear all the pims on the field to clear a round.

About us

"Stuck in Pimbo" was created by Ana & Alex—Team Huggabugga. This is our second Ludum Dare game, but our first completed in time for the Jam. The idea for "Stuck in Pimbo" came from the desire to develop a game that didn't involve violence. Instead, you're playing God and resurrecting the dead. Hope you enjoy it!
